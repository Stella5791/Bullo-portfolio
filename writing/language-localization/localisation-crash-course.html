Overview

TLDR

  • Localisation is more than translation.

I begin with purpose. I map audiences and constraints. I adapt not only text but flows, visuals, and support content. I document choices so teams can iterate with confidence.

Who this is for

Product managers, designers, and copy teams expanding to new markets.

What you will learn

  • What changes beyond wording
  • How to audit culture touchpoints
  • How to run lean market checks

Step by step

  1. Define success in the new locale.
  2. Audit content and flows.
  3. Adapt text, graphics, and support.
  4. Test with five users. Iterate.

Examples

Signup flows that need surname rules. Payment pages that need local options. Support content that needs tone changes.

Pitfalls

  • Literal translation
  • Ignoring legal norms
  • Inconsistent voice

Conclusion

When teams design for culture, adoption rises and support tickets fall.
