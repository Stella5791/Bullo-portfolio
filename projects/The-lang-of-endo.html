About the Project

The Language of Endometriosis explores how people describe pain in their own words‚Äî revealing not just symptoms, but lived experiences. This ongoing project brings together linguistics, health communication, and digital tools to better understand and communicate complex, invisible pain.

Initiated by Dr Stella Bullo, this work includes peer-reviewed research, public articles, a custom dataset, and a metaphor-tagging web app for patients and clinicians.
